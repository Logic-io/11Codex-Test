Sales Follow-up Workflow Prototype

Self-initiated workflow prototype based on real retail sales and customer consultation scenarios.

Overview

I designed this self-initiated prototype from real retail scenarios. It organizes customer details, follow-up, sales stages, and recurring questions without requiring a complex custom platform.

Workflow

  • Capture product, budget, timeline, objections, language, and next step.
  • Generate consistent AI-assisted follow-up and classify each customer stage.
  • Summarize common questions to improve FAQs, sales scripts, and content.

Tools / Skills

AI-Assisted Writing CRM Thinking Spreadsheet Workflow Customer Segmentation Sales Follow-up Reporting

Expected Value

More consistent follow-up, better customer organization, stronger sales scripts, and less repetitive admin work.
