AI Tools & Workflow Architecture

A practical approach to selecting and connecting AI tools for research, communication, coding, and repeatable work.

Overview

I use Gemini, ChatGPT, NotebookLM, and Codex as complementary tools rather than interchangeable chatbots. Each tool supports a defined stage, with clear inputs, reusable instructions, human review, and a practical final output.

Use Architecture

  • Gemini: explore information, compare sources, and work with multimodal context.
  • ChatGPT: structure ideas, refine communication, and develop reusable prompts or content.
  • NotebookLM: organize source materials, generate grounded summaries, and trace insights back to references.
  • Codex: inspect files, edit code, test changes, and turn plans into working digital outputs.
  • Workflow: define the goal, gather context, select the tool, review the result, and store reusable knowledge.

Business Value

Faster research and execution, more consistent communication, less repetitive work, and a clearer path from idea to usable output.
