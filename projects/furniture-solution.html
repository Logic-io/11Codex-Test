Design & Visual Communication

A case study in using visual judgment, AI-assisted ideation, and audience needs to make products, training, and events easier to understand.

Overview

I use Canva, AI-assisted ideation, audience personas, space planning, and product knowledge to create clearer visual communication for customers and partner teams.

Approach

  • Create product photos, tags, and social materials with consistent visual presentation.
  • Use AI-assisted ideation and audience personas to design training materials and event flyers that help partner teams understand key messages.
  • Understand room size, lifestyle, design preference, budget, and buying motivation.
  • Connect visual options with practical advice using Canva and 20-20 Design experience.

Tools / Skills

Canva AI-Assisted Ideation Persona-Based Design Training & Event Flyers Visual Presentation Space Planning Furniture Consultation 20-20 Design

Business Value

Clearer product presentation, stronger partner understanding, and more accessible training and event communication.
