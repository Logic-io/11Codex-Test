Design & Visual Communication

A case study in using visual judgment, product presentation, and customer needs analysis to make buying decisions easier.

Overview

I use Canva, space planning, product knowledge, and design-focused consultation to help furniture and kitchen customers connect style with space, lifestyle, budget, and practical use.

Approach

  • Create product photos, tags, and social materials with consistent visual presentation.
  • Understand room size, lifestyle, design preference, budget, and buying motivation.
  • Connect visual options with practical advice using Canva and 20-20 Design experience.

Tools / Skills

Canva Visual Presentation Space Planning Furniture Consultation 20-20 Design

Business Value

Stronger product presentation and a clearer buying experience that balances design preference with practical needs.
