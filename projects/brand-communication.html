Dell Brand Project

Dell Store Winner Boost event 2026

A store winner recognition and training activation project designed to celebrate retail performance, energize the team, and connect Dell product positioning with a memorable in-store experience.

Campaign Design Planning Execution Training Activation

Concept

I designed the flyer and campaign concept around an original sci-fi light-versus-dark theme. The Light Side represented cleaner energy options such as high-protein bars, fresh fruit, and sparkling water. The Dark Side represented high-sugar and high-calorie boost options for peak traffic moments.

The idea was inspired by familiar space-opera storytelling, but rebuilt with original wording, layout, and visual direction to keep the campaign distinct and brand-safe.

Role & Execution

  • Designed the campaign flyer and visual concept.
  • Planned the store winner boost and team activation setup.
  • Organized the light-side and dark-side refreshment categories.
  • Supported team communication, recognition, and training discussion around Dell customer engagement.
