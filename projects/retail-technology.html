Retail Technology & Product Consultation

A case study in translating technical product details into clear customer value during retail technology conversations.

Project Overview

This project demonstrates how I approach customer-facing product explanation, needs analysis, product comparison, budget/value communication, and Dell retail technology consultation.

Context

Retail technology customers often compare laptops, monitors, gaming PCs, storage, display quality, performance, and price while trying to understand which option fits their real use case.

Challenge

The challenge is to simplify technical specifications without losing accuracy, while helping customers feel confident instead of overwhelmed by competing product options.

My Role

As a Dell Brand Ambassador in a Best Buy retail environment, I support product education, in-store consultation, team communication, customer feedback, and product comparison conversations.

Actions

  • Asked customer questions about use case, performance needs, budget, timeline, and buying concerns.
  • Explained laptops, monitors, gaming PCs, and technology solutions in plain customer-friendly language.
  • Compared specs, storage, display quality, performance, value, and practical fit.
  • Collaborated with retail teams to support product knowledge, sales strategies, and Dell solution presentation.
  • Communicated customer feedback, traffic insights, product interest, and sales opportunities.

Tools / Skills

Consultative Sales Product Demonstration Retail Technology Customer Needs Analysis Objection Handling Team Communication

Outcome / Business Value

The business value is stronger product understanding, clearer customer conversations, better comparison support, and more confident retail purchase decisions.
